Privacy Policy

AVSysMaster · Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AVSysMaster (“the App”) handles information. The App is an iPad control surface used to communicate with professional audio/video equipment over a local network via TCP.

1. Summary

  • We do not require an account or login.
  • We do not collect personal information for advertising or analytics.
  • We do not sell or share user data with third parties.
  • Configuration and logs stay on your device unless you export them yourself.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following locally on your iPad:

  • Control panel configuration (devices, IP addresses, ports, commands, layouts, themes)
  • Custom images you choose (logo / background)
  • Operation logs of commands sent from the App

This data is saved in the App’s local storage (Application Support) and is not uploaded to our servers. The App does not operate a backend that receives your content.

3. Local Network Access

The App requests Local Network permission so it can send TCP commands to AV devices (for example matrix switchers, DSPs, or displays) that you configure. Network traffic goes only to the hosts and ports you enter. We do not use Local Network access to scan or collect unrelated personal data.

4. Import / Export

You may export or import configuration as JSON files using the system Files / Share sheet. You decide where those files are stored or sent. We do not receive them.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social SDKs that collect personal data.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for professional AV use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete it within the App, replace it by importing a new configuration, or remove the App. Uninstalling the App removes its local data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue at github.com/blueflysky/AVSysMaster/issues or contact the developer via GitHub: @blueflysky.
